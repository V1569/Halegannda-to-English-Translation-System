Why Halegannada Matters

Halegannada, or Old Kannada, is the ancestor of the modern Kannada language. It was predominantly used between the 6th and 14th centuries CE. This ancient form of Kannada has given us countless literary masterpieces including works by renowned poets like Pampa, Ranna, and Ponna.

Our translation tools help bridge the gap between this rich historical language and modern readers, making centuries of wisdom and literature accessible to everyone.
